## 2. Problem 区

### 目标

讲痛点，别一上来讲技术。

### 标题

Player effort disappears too easily

### 文案

In `EVE Frontier`, players explore, build, fight, transport, and support a living world. But most of that effort remains fragmented inside logs, isolated interfaces, or short-lived moments that are hard to verify and even harder to share.

### 三个问题点

- Meaningful activity is hard to translate into visible reputation
- Contribution is difficult to prove outside the game context
- Social sharing lacks a verifiable identity layer

---

## 3. Solution 区

### 目标

把产品主线讲明白。

### 标题

From activity to honor

### 核心文案

`EVE Medals` connects indexed Frontier activity, achievement verification, on-chain medal ownership, and social-ready share cards into one product flow.

### 四步流程

1. Index Frontier activity  
   Chronicle reads meaningful player actions from the indexed activity window.

2. Verify achievement state  
   The system maps those actions into medal progress and readiness states.

3. Claim on-chain honor  
   Eligible medals can be claimed and bound to the player wallet on Sui.

4. Share public reputation  
   Warrior profiles and medal cards turn achievement into a visible, portable identity asset.
