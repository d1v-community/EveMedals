# EVE Medals 官网 About 文案

## 版本一：标准版

### About EVE Medals

`EVE Medals` 是一个面向 `EVE Frontier` 玩家成就验证与社交传播的产品。

我们将玩家在 Frontier 世界中的关键行为转化为可验证的勋章状态、链上荣誉凭证和可分享的 Warrior 卡片，让战绩不再只是一次短暂记录，而是能够沉淀、展示和传播的身份资产。

在 EVE Frontier 这样的世界里，玩家投入了大量时间去探索、建设、战斗和协作，但这些行为往往缺少一种长期可见、可验证、可传播的荣誉表达方式。  
`EVE Medals` 想解决的，就是这件事。

通过接入行为索引与链上勋章机制，我们把玩家的 Frontier 行为整理成可理解的成就快照，并在满足条件后生成可领取的勋章。玩家不仅可以在自己的 Warrior 页面中展示这些荣誉，还可以把它们分享成社交卡片，让外部世界看见这些真实发生过的 Frontier 故事。

这不仅仅是在“发一个 NFT”。  
我们更关心的是，把游戏行为、链上证明和社交表达打通，让玩家获得更强的身份认同，也让每一枚勋章都具备传播价值。

当前，`EVE Medals` 已经具备成就验证、勋章领取、Warrior 展示和分享卡片能力，并正在继续补齐更完整的传播归因与增长机制。

我们的目标很简单：

> 让 EVE Frontier 玩家的荣誉被看见、被验证、被传播。

## 版本二：更品牌化一点

### About

`EVE Medals` helps turn Frontier activity into visible reputation.

We build a system that captures meaningful player actions, translates them into verifiable achievement states, and expresses them through on-chain medals, Warrior profiles, and shareable cards.

In most games, effort disappears too quickly.  
You fight, build, travel, trade, and contribute to a living world, but the proof of that effort is fragmented, temporary, or hard to share.

`EVE Medals` is designed to make that effort legible.

By combining indexed activity data with on-chain medal ownership, we create a layer of identity around what players have actually done in `EVE Frontier`. The result is not just a collectible, but a public-facing proof of contribution that can live inside the product and travel across social platforms.

Our long-term vision is to turn achievement into reputation, and reputation into distribution.

### About EVE Medals

`EVE Medals` is an achievement verification and social identity layer for `EVE Frontier`.

We transform meaningful Frontier activity into verifiable medal states, on-chain honors, and shareable Warrior cards, so player effort can become something visible, portable, and socially expressive.

Today, the product already supports achievement verification, medal claiming, Warrior profile display, and shareable card generation. Over time, it will continue evolving into a stronger social distribution and growth layer built on top of verifiable player identity.
